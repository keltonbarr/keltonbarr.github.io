Initial Planning Document

Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is to create a working weather website for three different locations. This developed site will bring together different information for the locations of Franklin, ID as well as Greenville, SC and Springfield, IL. The site will display the location, high and low temperatures, precipitation chance, wind speed and direction, and a summary of the weather experienced. Important information about weather in all locations is important as well as an easy to look at display. Information should be displayed simply and effectively so that visitors of the site can rapidly get their information they are searching for and will desire to come back again.

Site Audience

The people using this site will be a large population. It will reach out to people from 15 to 75 who have working computers, tablets, or mobile phone devices. The aim is to assist all who have access to internet to know the weather they will expereince when the go about their daily work or visit a new location or go on vacation. Many like to know the weather they will face to get ready for the day or for the trip. To use the site effectively will take little to no previous experience with the website at all. This could be used by a single man or woman or even a family that is planning a trip or even just going to the store or a park. This site can be used in bed, at a desktop computer, on the go or wherever wifi is accessable.

Persona

Stephen is a business man and a father. He lives in a relatively small town and has to travel long distances to get to work or to get home after. Many days his travel is done by bicycle and he very rarely uses a car. The thing he always worries most about in his daily travel is the weather. He likes to make sure if he will be biking that day that he will not have a run in with bad weather or anything he did not expect. Depending on the situation he may have different devices from his personal cell phone or tablet when he is at home or his desktop computer at work. He checks the weather regulary to ensure comfortable and safe travel.
